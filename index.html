Circular Economy

Nothing Goes
to Waste.

Every 1000 kg of tea served by our vendor network produces used tea waste — rich in nitrogen, tannins and organic matter. Instead of discarding it, we've built a reverse logistics system to collect, process and return value to the earth.

DIRAAZ delivery vehicles collect vendor waste on their return trips. Small incentive discounts encourage participation. A simple, elegant, circular loop.

1
Vendor Collection

Labelled buckets at each vendor. Pickup within 24hrs of use. Drain liquid, short sun-dry.

2
Processing at Base

Sort, pre-treat, mix with carbon material. Prepare for downstream pathways.

3
Value Recovery

Vermicompost sold to nurseries & farms. Animal feed pilot with dairy cooperatives. Biogas partnerships in planning.

Vermicompost

Easiest entry. Sell to nurseries & organic farms at ₹15–40/kg. Lucknow buyers already exist.

Animal Feed

Dried spent leaves as 5–10% feed supplement. Milk residue improves palatability. Dairy belt opportunity.

Biogas

Wet milk-waste is ideal anaerobic digester feedstock. Partner with local dairy plants — longer term.

Zero to Landfill

Our goal: every kg of tea served creates zero landfill waste. Tea as a fully circular product.
